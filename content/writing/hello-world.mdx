This is the first post on this site. I built it because I wanted a clean, fast, and minimal place to publish essays and notes.

## Why build your own?

There's no shortage of platforms — Medium, Substack, Ghost, WordPress. But I wanted full control over the design, no analytics tracking, and the ability to write in Markdown with components.

<Callout type="note" title="Tech Stack">
This site is built with Next.js, TypeScript, SCSS, and MDX. It's deployed on Vercel and scores well on Lighthouse.
</Callout>

## The stack

Here's what powers this site:

- **Next.js** (App Router) for routing and static generation
- **TypeScript** for type safety
- **SCSS Modules** for scoped, maintainable styles
- **MDX** for writing with React components inline
- **Vercel** for deployment

## What I'll write about

I plan to write about technology, design, and the process of building things. Some posts will be short notes, others will be longer essays.
